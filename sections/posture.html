Posture: consolidate or distribute?

The Army holds a fixed amount of forward surgery. It can consolidate that capacity into a few large hospitals or distribute it into many small forward teams. Consolidation pools servers: a larger team idles less between cases and works off a surge faster. Distribution shortens the trip: the wounded travel less distance to a table. Which one saves more lives depends on whether the Army holds air superiority.
